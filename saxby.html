Executive Summary

Saxby is the UK trade electrician's everyday brand — with deep wholesale distribution and formidable range depth. Part of Poole Lighting Ltd (also owns Endon), Saxby has 30+ years manufacturing history and claims 98% stock holding with next-day delivery. Their fire-rated downlight ranges (ShieldECO for wholesale, CosmosEco for Screwfix) are specification-driven: fire-rated 30/60/90 min, IP65, dimmable, CCT selectable, tool-less push-fit installation. Multi-retailer sweep confirms stocking at 15+ online retailers across trade and retail channels.

Distribution spans wholesale and retail. Wholesale-first via CEF, Edmundson, Rexel, YESSS Electrical, and City Electrical Factors. Retail presence at Screwfix (75 downlights, 61 outdoor wall lights, 73 indoor products listed — Feb 2026). Also stocked at Electrical Counter, Click4Electrics, Electrical4Less, Sparks Direct, Alert Electrical, Electrical2Go, Lamplec, Direct Trade Supplies, Lights4Living, and Amazon UK. This multi-channel reach is a core strength, though price variance is significant: HeroPRO bulkhead ranges from ~£10 (Lamplec/Click4Electrics trade) to £22 (Electrical Counter).

Channel-specific branding: Screwfix sells the CosmosEco V3 range (top-selling Saxby downlight on Screwfix, 4.9★, £8.89 single chrome), while wholesale channels carry the ShieldECO range. Both are fire-rated IP65 with similar specs, but different naming may cause confusion. The ShieldSLIM (103174) is the slim-profile play — 7W 4CCT (2700/3000/4000/6500K) designed for shallow ceiling voids. The OrbitalPRO (102669) steps up to 9W for higher-output applications.

Outdoor: Oslo outsells Palin at Screwfix. The Oslo PIR Grey (£36.99, 4.9★ from 9 reviews) is Screwfix's #1 Saxby outdoor wall light, followed by Icarus PIR (£14.99) and Arc Up & Down (£12.98). The Palin marine grade (316 SS) range offers IP65 coastal specification with PIR options.

Key gaps vs design brands: No pendant range. No decorative table/floor lamps. No smart home integration. No Scandinavian design cachet. But within its trade lane, Saxby's range depth is formidable: the Electrical Counter alone lists 15+ downlight sub-ranges (ShieldECO, ShieldPRO, OrbitalPRO, SirioDISC, Speculo, Garrix, Hoxton, Ravel, Axial, Cast, Converse, Heatguard, LALO, Pacto, Peake, Tetra, Xeno).

Market Dynamics & Commercial Insights

7 key findings

CosmosEco V3 Is Screwfix's #1 Saxby Downlight

Screwfix doesn't stock ShieldECO — they sell CosmosEco V3 (4.9★, £8.89 chrome single). This channel-specific branding means the same manufacturer has different product lines for different retailers.

→ Channel-specific branding creates market complexity for competitors.

121% Price Spread on Same Product

HeroPRO bulkhead 95539: Lamplec £9.99 vs Electrical Counter £22.08. IkonPRO decking: Alert £40.01 vs EC £54.00 (35%). ShieldECO Satin Nickel: EC special £5.70 vs standard £6.84. This spread rewards procurement research.

→ Trade pricing intelligence is a competitive advantage.

Warranty Registration Creates Trade Friction

Extended warranty (3yr/5yr) requires online registration with proof of purchase AND NAPIT/NICEIC licence number. Electricians' forums describe this as discriminatory. Standard warranty is only 2 years.

→ Simple, no-registration warranties would differentiate immediately.

Oslo Outsells Palin at Screwfix

Oslo PIR Grey (£36.99, 4.9★, 9 reviews) is #1 Saxby outdoor wall light at Screwfix. The Palin marine-grade range — ostensibly the flagship — ranks lower. Entry-priced Arc (£12.98) and Icarus (£14.99) also rank highly.

→ Value-priced outdoor wall lights drive volume, not premium marine-grade.

ShieldSLIM Solves Shallow Ceiling Voids

Slim-profile 7W fire-rated downlight with 4CCT selection (2700/3000/4000/6500K). Designed specifically for shallow ceiling voids where standard-depth downlights won't fit — one of the most common installation headaches for electricians on refurb projects.

→ Products that solve real installation pain points drive trade loyalty.

15+ Downlight Sub-Ranges Create Confusion

Electrical Counter lists ShieldECO, ShieldPRO, OrbitalPRO, SirioDISC, Speculo, Garrix, Hoxton, Ravel, Axial, Cast, Converse, Heatguard, LALO, Pacto, Peake, Tetra, Xeno — plus CosmosEco at Screwfix. Range proliferation may confuse specifiers.

→ A simplified, clearly differentiated range would be easier to specify.

Guard Floodlight = 2025/26 Growth Play

New floodlight range (20/30/50/100/200W) in die-cast aluminium, IP65, with PIR override options. Available at 8+ online retailers. 3-year warranty (2+1 extended). Extends Saxby's outdoor/commercial footprint beyond bulkheads.

→ Saxby is actively expanding outdoor/commercial categories.
